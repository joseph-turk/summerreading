Kids & Teen Summer Library Club
Sign Up FAQs

When does registration open and close?
Registration will open Saturday, May 26th at 9:30am. Registration will be open all summer. If an event is full, you can sign up for the waitlist.
Where do I register?
You can register online, over the phone, or at the library. You can access the online registration from the library website, our Facebook page, and on this website.
How long do I have to complete the online registration?
You will not be timed out of the registration site, but your spot will not be reserved until you click “register”.
How can I tell if an event has open spots?
Just visit the Kids or Teens registration page and you’ll see how many spots are available for each event.
How will I know if I’m moved off the waitlist and able to attend an event?
You will receive a call or email from library staff if a spot opens up for you.
What do I do if I need to cancel or change my reservation?
Call the library at 608-882-2260 and we will be happy to update your registration.
What should I do if never received a confirmation email or I forgot what I signed up for?
Check your junk or spam folder for an email from registrations@efplsummersignup.com. If you still need help, call the library at 608-882-2260.
Where are these events located?
All registered events will be held at the Evansville Youth Center located at 209 S 1st St. Evansville, WI 53536.
